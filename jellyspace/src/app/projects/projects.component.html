How-To guide for posting a Project?

1) Give your Project a clear and descriptive title for understandability.
2) Describe your Project Requirements and be as detailed as possible .
3) Optionally you can upload files (ex: PDFs, Images, or Word Documents for Architecture, Design, Development, Test etc.)
4) Add up to five skills that are necessary to complete the undertaken Project. These will help our AI Recommender System Engine to reach-out and match-make relevant talented personnel for your assignment.
5) Select the Project payment category type (ex: Fixed-Pricing, Hourly, or budget based etc.)
6) Add optional Project categories (ex: Featured, Urgent, Private i.e. Selected Individuals only, Sealed i.e. bidding is not disclosed to others, Non-Disclosure Agreement - NDA, Intellectual Property - IP Agreement, Full-time)
